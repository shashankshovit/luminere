Mind Tester

OVERVIEW:

Are you good at the basics of physics? Then here is your chance to prove yourself. Physics Association presents a funda packed conceptual physics quiz having multiple rounds including elimination round, audio visual rounds, rapid-fire and finale. Addressing a large number of conceptual and mindboggling thoughts, this one day event is sure to excite all you physics lovers out there.
RULES:-
·Teams of max two are allowed.
· After an elimination round, the best performing teams will be selected for further rounds and finally two teams will compete in the finale.
