Puzzled

Overview

As fast as an eagle, as slow as a slug. As tall as a tree, as small as a bug. Sometimes in front, sometimes behind. Never on top, and always aligned. Just let your bulbs glow, and you will see its your “shadow”.
Since its not always about scratching your brains till question marks fly off from paper to head, it might not be about rubik cubes, sudokus, hanoi towers , nonograms or any donkey work. So as we put the brainy minds to rest in peace , the distracted ones can pop out alive. Finally with lots of fun loving puzzles and a belief that art of simplicity is a puzzle of complexity, we present to you PUZZLED

Rule

• Participation- maximum of two thinking heads.
• All the inventory required will be provided.
• All the correct answers will earn the number of points indicated for the respective puzzles.
• All answers must be submitted within the 2 hours time limit. Late submissions will be penalized 2 points per minute after the deadline.
• Ranking and tie-breaking will be based on the following rules in order:
          1. Most total points
          2. Earliest submission time
• The judgement of the judge(s) shall be final.
