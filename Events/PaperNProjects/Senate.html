SENATE

OVERVIEW:
~ A Call to the Youth of Nation ~
An event aimed at directing the focus of Indian youth at the various problems that India is currently facing and to find possible solutions to those problems. The Event is in two phases: Manthan & Samadhaan. (The medium of the presentation can be any one of Hindi or English or both. Please see the rules and judging criteria under their respective tabs) RULES:-
PHASE 1: MANTHAN - Eliminations
· Group discussions on a general topic, consisting of 6-8 participants each.
· The duration will be 5 minutes to think on the given topic followed by 15 minutes for group discussion.
.The Discussion will be presided over by a Moderator/Judge who will ensure a smooth and meaningful discussion.
· The participants will be evaluated on their skills.
· Five participants will qualify for the second phase. 
PHASE 2: SAMAADHAN - Competition among selected participants
· The selected participants will be given different issues/ problems just after the completion of Phase 1.
· Putting themselves in the position of a leader and a policy-maker, they will be asked to come out with the complete scenario and solution to the problem allotted to them.
· The participants can make use of audio/visual aids but they must be strictly confined to figures and statistics.
· Expected duration: 10 minutes per participant for presenting followed by question and answers for a maximum of 5 minutes more (approx. 15 minutes per participant)
JUDGING CRITERIA:-
· Content of the presentation
· Communication skills
· Leadership Skills
· Feasibility of Solution
.Innovation in the Solution
