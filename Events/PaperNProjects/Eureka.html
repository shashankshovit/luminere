Introduction

Present yourself in front of judges and students about your research work and technical topic within time limit. Here , We provide you a National level plateform for showing your skills. Receiving and selecting of abstract and gathering for presentation are steps for this event.

Details and Rules:


1. A maximum of 3 participants are allowed per team all of them belonging to the same institute. No entrant can be a part of more than one team. There is no limitation on the number of teams from an institute at any stage of the competition.
2. Papers to be presented at LUMINERE’11 will be selected only on the basis of the originality.
3. The screening will be done on the basis of the abstract.The abstract should be mailed to analysisppt@yahoo.com on or before 12th Novembor, 2011.
4. Partcipants have to specify the category in which they want to present paper. If one is presenting paper from ampere category,he has to write subject name as "ampere_(team name)".
5. Participants should send their name , college id, college name,phone number along with their abstract.
6. Acknowledgement of the receipt of the entries would be sent through email.
7. Participants whose abstract will be short listed shall be informed personally through emails and phone on or before 13 November 2011.
8. Selected participants will have to give their presentations in Microsoft PowerPoint.
9. Each team will get a maximum of 10 minutes for presentation and 2 minutes for queries. If exceeded, the marks may be deducted accordingly.
10. A presentation has to be created by the participants based on your paper and that would be presented before the experts. The entire report/paper in form of three hard copies and one soft copy should be brought to the venue for judges.
11. Audio /video are strictly prohibited. Pictures and Graph may be used.
12. Decision of the judges will be final.
