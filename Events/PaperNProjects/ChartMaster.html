CHART MASTER

Introduction :

The main objectives of the ‘Chart Competition’ is to build team spirit. Confine your knowledge in a chart and present it in front of judges and students. A platform is provided here for showing your technical skills and knowledge.

Details:


Participants may use tag,flowing diagram,bar diagram and figure in their chart.
Participants should be ready for answering the questions of judges related to their chart at the time of exhibition.

Rules:

1. Maximum 3 members per team.
2. Technical topics are allowed.
3. Participants must bring the prepared chart with themselves .
4. Verdict of the judges will be final.
5. Prizes and ceritificates will be awarded to the students of first , second and third position.
