Portfolio

Overview

photographer’s portfolio refers to his best work, the face of it all. So here’s your chance to make your portfolio speak volumes of your work, your essence. The participants are required to submit their best work of Nature, Portrait and Product. Prize winning entries will be showcased during Photography Club’s APOGEE exhibition.

Rule


• Participants have to submit one picture in each category: Nature, Portrait, Product.

• The final submitted entry should be the original work of the participant. • Fine touching of the pictures is permissible.

• Participants are requested not to mention their names on the picture, or add borders, violation of which will lead to disqualification.
br>• The picture must be in JPEG format with a minimum size of 1280 x 800 pixels and a resolution of 300 dpi.

• For online participation, picture name should of the format “NAME OF THE CANDIDATE_COLLEGE_CATEGORY.jpeg”. The candidates should mail the pictures latest by 20 March, 2011, 2359 hours to photog.bits@gmail.com, with the subject Portfolio 2011.

• For offline participation, the participants are required to submit hard copies (A4 SIZE, 8x12 in) of their entries in the Photog Room, after verification of their registration on the campus, latest by 5 p.m., 26th March. The participants are required to mention their name, the college’s name and the category behind every picture.

• The decision of the judges will be final and binding on the participants.

• Winner's details will be verified before declaring the results of the competition.

• Decision of the Photography Club post holders regarding any ambiguities will be binding and final.

Judging

1. Aesthetic sense of the picture in each category
2. Pictures will be disqualified for addition of borders or use of post processing techniques to add new elements to the picture.
3. Marks will be deducted for over-post processing in nature and portraits.
