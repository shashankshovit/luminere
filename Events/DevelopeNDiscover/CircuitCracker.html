Circuit Cracker

Introduction

Resolve the mystery of brain teaser in minimum time and the one with best short and innovative circuit wins... so buck up for the new venture starting this October

Details:

In this, you have to unravel the circuit-puzzle and make an experimental model of the solution on the Bread board. The one who come up with the best possible solution and make the experimental model in the shortest time will be declared as winner.

Rules :

1. The team will comprise of two members and the event will be held in two rounds.
2. Prelims-objective quiz related to electrical and electronics stream.
3. Finals -basic electronic kit will be provided and finalists will be asked to design a circuit on the BreadBoard as per the given problem statement.
4. Time taken to design the correct circuit will be the judging criteria.
5. Judges decision will be final.
