Code Hex

Introduction

Are you good enough in assembly programming.if yes the,this is all about microprocessor 8085 and 8086..so just retrieve your knowledge jump into the race

Details:

Event will be in two rounds.
First round is a paper-pen round about microprocessor 8085 and 8086.
12 top students will be selected for 2nd round after 1st round
In second round participants have to make a program on microprocessor 8085 & 8086 simulator..

Rules :

For 1st Participants will be given a objective question paper based on microprocessor 8085 for round 1 and they have to solve it in question paper itself.
For 2nd round each participant will be given a different program and he/she have to execute the program on 8085 or 8086 simulator accordingly..
The participants will be given same simulator.

JUDGING CRITERIA:

One who executes its program early,will be awarded bonus points if submitted correctly. The team with maximum marks will be the winner.
