Break D Break

Introduction

Why do hens sit on their eggs? Is it to prevent them from breaking? Are you ready to do what ever it takes to save a falling egg from breaking?

Details:

Description :-

The objective of this competition is to protect an egg from breaking when dropped from a given height. Each team has to design a device which can resist the shock of falling and prevent the egg from breaking.

Guidelines :-

It is an on-the-spot event. Each team will get the following items:

Set-I


1 - Piece of string, 30 feet long.
1 - Piece of foam board, 6 inches X 6 inches
1 - Role of small-size adhesive tape
1 - Project description sheet
1 - Tube fevicol
A virtual credit of 1000 points

Set-II

Apart from set-I, certain additional items will be available. Each item will be assigned certain points. A team trades their allotted points for the items For example- If Sponge (300 points) Newspaper(200 points) Marble balls(150 points) are the items that are available and a team is interested in using 2 sponges and a newspaper, they will be provided the items and 800 points(2x300 + 1x200) will be deducted from their virtual credit. This remaining credit will be a criteria for judging.

Rules :

General Rules :


1.Entrants may be an individual or a team(max 3 members).
2.Contestants must be present at the end of the contest to win.
3. Anyone with an imagination is eligible to enter this contest.
4. Eggs will be provided on the day of the contest.
5.The contestants have to place the egg inside their device.
6.Height of fall will be measured from the bottom most point of the device.
7.The Organizers have the final determination in all matters.
8. Follow common rules of safety at the event. No running or rough housing please.
9.Conditions and rules are subjected to change till before the competition.
