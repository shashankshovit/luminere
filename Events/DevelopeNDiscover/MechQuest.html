Mech Quest

Introduction

"Quality is never an accident
It is always the result of intelligent"
 So bring your intelligence to test the mechanical knowledge buried beneath the nerves of your brain and get ready to face some mind-boggling problems!!!!!!

Details:

The event comprises of two rounds.

ROUND 1:

This round will test your mind to solve mechanical riddles and pictures to reach to the answer.

ROUND 2:

1. In this round you have to identify mechanical parts in a given list (out of a list of 3) and write them down in a given duration of time.
2. If you identify all your parts correctly and still have time left you can go to identify bonus parts.

Rules :

1. The team may consist of 2 members only.
2. No participant can be part of more than one team.
3. The teams qualifying round 1 will be eligible to participate in round 2.
4. Any sort of misbehaviour observed with the judges or organisers or any other competing team will staright away lead to disqualification.
5. In case of a tie judges decision will be the last and will not be questioned.
6. The organizers reserve the right to change/update the rules of the contest at any point of time and will do their best to inform the participants same. However, it is ultimately the responsibility of the teams to keep themselves updated.
