Solstice

Introduction

A chance to polish your innovative skills... Let’s concentrate solar heat energy using mirrors or aluminium sheet(s) to raise the temperature of the given fluid.

Details:

EVENT GUIDELINES :-

1. Each team can comprise of max 3 members.
2. Design any reflector or a parabolic concentrator of your desired dimensions either by using mirrors or aluminum sheet(s) etc.
3. Half litre of fluid e.g. water will be provided to trap concentrated heat.
4. Design a collector of any dimension to accommodate given quantity of fluid.
5.Whole assembly should be as simple as possible.

Rules :

General Rules :

1. Concentrator will be kept for 30 minutes under sun light or 100 W incandescent bulb which depends on the weather.
2. Participants will not be allowed to change or touch the concentrator after fixing up the setup.
3. After 30 minutes the temperature of water will be noted down.
4. Result will be concluded on the basis of water temperature which would be duly considered in case of temperature variations.
