Mason Master

Introduction

AN EVENT TO FIND YOUR ABILITY AS A MASON
(Not just for civil engg and architect, students of every stream can participate in this event)

Mere watching, from your nearby structures and a little knowledge of materials, you may find it easy to construct a wall, and if u think so then try it in mason masters.. This is a platform for you to try your hand over brick and cement.

Details:

Judgement Criteria:

1.Height of the wall (as provided in the concerning paper)
2.Proper right angles need to be maintained between the two adjoining walls.
3.Verticality of the wall
4.Joints of the Bricks.

EVENT GUIDELINES :-

1. Show your skill as a mason and general knowledge of materials.
2. You will be provided bricks, cement, trowel, sand, plumb bob and set square.
3. You will have to construct a wall of given dimensions in the given time. A wall needs to be design with good finishing.
4. Show your skill as a good planning engineer in making of wall.

Rules :

A team should have 3 members.

General Rules :

1. Team should have 3 members.
2. Event will be conducted in two rounds.
ROUND 1
1. It will be a test for just 30 minutes, in which every team of 3 members will be given a Question paper.
2. Question paper will contain 75 no. of objective questions in three sets (each containing 25 questions).
3. Paper can be solved by one set each member or by mutual understanding of three (taking 3 sets together).
4. Questions will be based simply on your general knowledge of materials, masonry, and mechanics.
ROUND 2:-
1. Winning 15 teams of Round 1 would be able to participate in this Round.
2. You will have to make a 'L- shape' wall of given dimensions in a given time limit of '1 Hour'.
3. The wall must be made of either Flemish bond or English bond. (Teams will be provided with a paper showing kind of bonds to know about the same.... helpful for the students of other stream)
4. The wall will be one and half(1 1/2) brick wall
5. Wall should be vertical with parallel faces and its joints should be perfect.
6. All the required tools and accessories will be provided to the contestants
