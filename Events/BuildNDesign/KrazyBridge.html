Krazy Bridge

Introduction

A perfect platform to manifest your creative skills and logical thinking to materialize a bridge that endures the maximum load to weight ratio.

Details:

Problem statement :-


Design a bridge model having dimensions given below. Materials will be provided on the spot.

Dimensions:-


-Length (span): 50 cm.
-Width: 10 cm
There should not be any variations in the dimensions of the bridge, Limiting to an error of 1 cm in width and 2 cm in length.

Test Conditions:- :

1.The Bridge will be kept on a platform which will be available on the spot.
2.A bucket will be tied to the centre of the bridge using a rope and sand will be filled slowly in to the bucket. The weight at which the bridge breaks will be calculated using an electronic balance.
3.A bucket will be tied to the centre of the bridge using a rope and sand will be filled slowly in to the bucket. The weight at which the bridge breaks will be calculated using an electronic balance.

Rules :

1. Maximum 3 Members per team. Participants from different institutions can form a team.
2. Adhesives and Popsicle sticks will be provided on the spot.
3.It is not compulsory to use all the sticks.
4. Use of material other than given is not permitted.
5. Participants should bring their own scale, pencil n cutter which they require at the time of the event.

General Rules :

1.Shape: Structure could be of absolutely any shape satisfying the above constraints.
2.Once the structure is weighed, you are not allowed to modify your structure in any way.
3.If the structure fails to satisfy any of the above constraints then it will be summarily rejected.
4. Any structure that is not ready with in the time limit will be disqualified immediately.
5. Participants should bring their own scale, pencil and other items which they require at the time of the event.
6.Judges' decision shall be final and binding on all.
7.The organizers reserve all rights to change any or all of the above rules at any point of the event.

WINNING CRITERIA:

The lightest bridge (that meets the given constraints & dimensions) supporting maximum weight at centre will be declared as the winner.
