Pipe-O-Mania

Introduction

It's all about pipes and economical strategies. Are you ready to test your mettle as a pipe specialist with flair of potential economic strategist?? Can u emerge successful from such real life circumstances? Judge your caliber at Neuron '10 in "Pipe-O-Mania ".

Rules :

Team Specifications:-

A team may consist of a maximum of 3 members. Students from different educational institutes may form a team.

General Rules :

1. Team should have at most 3 members. Out of which one is the team leader.
2. Team members can be of different colleges.
3. No participant can be part of more than one team.
4. Event will be conducted in two rounds.
5. Violation of any rule can result in rejection.

ROUND 1

First round is preliminary elimination round:-
1. It will be a test for just 30 minutes, in which every team of 3 members will be given a Question paper.
2. This round contains 20 simple questions based on application of fluid mechanics (15 very simple type questions + 5 star mark questions)

ROUND 2

1. Winning 8 teams of Round 1 would be able to participate in this Round.
2. You will have to empty two tanks (tank-1 &tank-2) which will be placed at different heights ( H1 & H2) and at some distance apart into a tank (tank-3) which will be at ground level.
3. You will be provided with limited pipes of different sizes ( cross section area &length )
4. Participants are required to bring their own accessories & calculator.
5. All the required tools will be provided to the contestants.

Details:

Judging Criteria:-

1 Winner will be decided on the basis of maximum volume in fixed time in tank-3.
2 Final decision of event jury will be valid only.

EVENT GUIDELINES :-

Pipe-o-mania will require some basic knowledge of fluid mechanics & pipe networks. It will test your spirit as a pipe specialist with style of potential economic strategist.

Certificate Policy:-

Certificate of Excellence will be given to all the winners.

Note :- The organizers reserve the right to change/update the rules of the contest at any point of time and will do their best to inform the participants of the same. However, it is ultimately the responsibility of the teams to keep themselves updated.
