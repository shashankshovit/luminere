Main Gate Entrance

Introduction

Designing the main gate entrance is like giving a brief introduction of the ambience which you can fell inside that place when you enter. So designing the interrelationship of space & merging it with the structure at the back.

Details:

Judging Criteria:-

Model would be judged on these basis :
1. Height of the structure .
2. Structure which can withstand the maximum velocity of wind.

Rules :

General Rules :

1. You will be provided with a base of a distance of 30 cm, where you need to design the gate with the materials provided within that space.
2. The participating team can be comprised of three members.
3.You will be given time duration of 2 hours.
4.The students will be provided questions & materials on the spot.
