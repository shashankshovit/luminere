“BUILDING BLOCKS OF TOMMOROW”

With available commodities and under provided technical constraints, a model has to be made with the best appeal to the problem statement.

Rules :

General Rules :

Two A3 size model sheets of different width, two cutters & adhesive would be provided to the participants.

Details:

Judging Criteria :-

Model would be judged on these three basis:
• Height of the structure
• Structure which can withstand the maximum velocity of wind.
• The form chosen by the participants ie. appeal in the aesthetic sense. Time Allotted: 2 hours
