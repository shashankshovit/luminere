3. Event will be of two rounds

Round 1 (Elimination Round)

-Teams qualified in this round will proceed to Round 2.

Round 2 (Final round)

-Will be a coding round
-Compiler will be JDK1.6
4. In any dispute decision of the coordinators will be final.
