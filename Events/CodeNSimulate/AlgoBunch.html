Algo Bunch

Introduction

FIND YOURSELF IN A “cuckoo's nest”. A NEST WHERE ONLY THOSE PERSON ARE CAUGHT WHO ARE MAD ABOUT WRITING ALGORITHM . WE PROVIDE YOU AN EVENT THAT TEST YOUR INGUINITY OF HOW MUCH YOU HAVE THE ABILITY TO SOLVE THE ALGO RIDDLES . EACH AND EVERY PROBLEM WILL BE NEW TO YOU…

Details :

No. of rounds-2.
No. of participants- Maximum two member in each team
Time Duration - Max. 1 hour for both rounds.
ROUND 1
No. of Questions-12
Type of question - Every question contain a problem statement for which a suitable algorithm should be written in a proper way.
Eight teams will be selected to move into second round.
ROUND 2
Algo is given to all teams & they have to implement it on C/C++/java compiler.
You will be provided both operating systems- Windows and Ubuntu.
Three winners will be selected based on their performance.

Rules :

• Teams of maximum two shall participate.
• The contest is divided into two rounds: a screening round, and the final round.
• Round I will be a written round comprising of 12 subjective Questions.Every question contain a problem statement for which a suitable algorithm should be written in a proper way. Duration: Max. 1 hour.
• Round II is the final round in which a code will have to be implemented in c/c++ or java based on the algorithm given. Duration: Max. 1 hour.
• Only perfectly correct result will be awarded marks. Partial code will NOT be rewarded.
• Code will be judged on the basis of the time taken.
• The programming languages to be used in round II will be C/C++ or java.
