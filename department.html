About

Discription

-UCE, Kota is one of the pretigious technical institutes in north India. It is ranked among top technical institutes of Rajasthan. The institute was established in 1981 by Govt. of Rajasthan. Ealier, the institute was known as Engineering College Kota[ECK] , but with establishment of Rajasthan Technical University in the campus, it was renamed to University College of Engineering…
Since its inception, over 8000 engineers have graduated from here and been placed at prestigious designations in different industries.
The institute hosts seminars, symposia, short term courses, competitions and contests.
                  It is an institute poised for significant expansion of its acaedemics and research activities….

Mission

                  -To reorganize education and research in close interaction with industry with emphasis on the development of leadership qualities in enterning portals of the institute..
